# DEPRECATED — Phase 2 translation

The files in this folder are the English translation drafts for
*Sōkyū-ō Sōba Zenshū* (宗久翁相場全集), produced by Claude Opus 4.6
during Phase 2 of the workflow. They were translated *from* the
vision-LLM transcription in `../transcription-vision-llm/` — which
has itself since been deprecated in favor of mechanical OCR.

**As of 2026-04-15, superseded.**

The authoritative translation will live in `../../translation/`, produced
from the mechanical transcription in `../../transcription/`.

This folder is retained for historical reference: some phrasing,
structural choices, and scholarly framing may remain useful input to
the Phase 3 translation, but the Phase 2 drafts inherited uncertainty
and interpolation from their vision-LLM source transcription and
should not be cited as-is.
