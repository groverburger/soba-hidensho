# Pages 95–100: Publisher Advertisements and Colophon

## Pages 95–96 — Shingidō Book Catalog

> 信義堂發行相場圖書目録

### Catalog of Market Books Published by Shingidō

These two pages contain publisher's advertisements for other works by
Hayasaka Nikiku (早阪二菊), issued by Shingidō (信義堂) of
Kakigara-chō, Nihonbashi-ku, Tokyo.

**1. 罫線圖解** (*Keisen Zukai*, "Chart Illustration") — by Hayasaka
Nikiku. Revised edition. Full cloth binding, one volume. Chrysanthemum
format, 310 pages. List price: 3 yen. Special price: 2 yen 50 sen.
(Postage included.)

The advertisement quotes reviews from several newspapers and journals:

- **Tōyō Keizai Shinpō** (東洋經濟新報): "This book dissects the
  state of price fluctuations in both the rice and stock markets from
  the standpoint of social and economic theory, comparing each point
  against actual practice … dividing the whole into Economic Market
  Recognition, Chart-Based Market Recognition, and Zen-Based Market
  Recognition … and further treating stocks and rice separately,
  explicating the principles governing rise and fall in each.
  Particularly, the inclusion of the history of Tokyo stock and
  futures markets, together with various statistical tables, makes
  it supremely convenient for research."

- **Tōkyō Keizai Zasshi** (東京經濟雜誌): "A book of cultivation
  for those who would profit through speculation on the exchange …
  consisting of sections on economic, chart-based, and Zen-based
  market methods … richly aphoristic as a work of its kind, and
  not lacking in historical materials."

- **Osaka Mainichi Shinbun** (大阪毎日新聞): praises the book's
  three-method approach — economic analysis, chart analysis, and
  Zen-based observation for maintaining composure amid market
  turbulence.

- **Osaka Asahi Shinbun** (大阪朝日新聞): "A fine book that
  synthesizes and distills the methods of successful traders in
  Japan, Europe, and America."

- **Shimonoseki Jitsugyō Nippō** (下關實業日報): distinguishes the
  book from older works based on yin-yang and five-elements
  superstition, calling it "a systematic method of market
  observation adapted to the present age."

**2. 高低足取表** (*Kōtei Ashitori-hyō*, "High-Low Price-Movement
Tables") — by Hayasaka Nikiku. With photographic supplement. Western
binding, chrysanthemum format. 240 pages. Covers stocks and futures.
List price: 2 yen. (Postage included.)

Review from **Tōkyō Keizai Zasshi**: "As a certain market trader
says, 'reading books leads to confusion' — but as this book
explains, unless one has been confused and confused again until one
has passed through confusion entirely, one cannot become a truly
successful trader. To pass through confusion requires studying the
works of one's predecessors. This book expounds the true meaning
of the *Sōba Zanmai Den* ('Market Absorption Tradition') in which
Honma Sōkyū, the great merchant of Shōnai, discussed market
fluctuations from a Zen standpoint."

**3. 相場新罫線學** (*Sōba Shin-Keisen-Gaku*, "New Chart Science
for Markets") — by Hayasaka Nikiku. Forthcoming.

**4. 株式新罫線學** (*Kabushiki Shin-Keisen-Gaku*, "New Chart
Science for Stocks") — by Hayasaka Nikiku. Forthcoming.
