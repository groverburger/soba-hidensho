# Pages 91--94: Sakata Rice-Exchange Price Records (Continued) and Colophon

*[Interpretive. The OCR for these tabular pages is heavily degraded.
The translation below is reconstructed primarily from the original
manuscript images, cross-referenced against the OCR text. Price
figures, month names, and calendar annotations have been verified
against the images wherever legible; annotations in square brackets
mark remaining uncertainties.]*

These pages continue the historical price record (相場の運行,
*soba no unkō*) of the Sakata rice exchange that began on page 90.
Each entry records the year, the year-block direction (塞, *fusagari*)
and associated stem-month for the ceiling (甲, *kinoe*), the opening
price of new contracts, notable monthly prices, and the magnitude of
major swings (expressed per 100 ryō of trading capital).

## Page 94 (right leaf) -- Price Records: Kansei 8 (continued) and Explanatory Note

*Kansei 8 continued:*

- **4th month, 29th day**: 22 tawara, 7 bu -- 36-tawara decline (三十六俵下げ)
- **7th month, 19th day**: 22 tawara, 4 bu -- *ushi*-day *tsuchinoto* rise (丑の日己上げ)
- **Intercalary 7th month** (閏七月): [record ends]

**Explanatory note** (釋, *shaku*):

> The foregoing records the price movements of the Sakata rice
> exchange. In the lower line, annotations such as "*kinoe* ceiling"
> (甲天井) or "*hinoe* ceiling" (丙天井) mean that the market reached
> its ceiling in a *kinoe*-month or a *hinoe*-month, respectively.
> The figures "rise of one hundred and some-odd tawara" (百何十俵上)
> that follow are stated per 100 ryō of capital.
