# DEPRECATED — Phase 2 translation

The files in this folder are the English translation drafts produced by
Claude Opus 4.6 during Phase 2 of the workflow. They were translated
*from* the vision-LLM transcription in
`../transcription-vision-llm/` — which has itself since been deprecated
in favor of mechanical OCR.

**As of 2026-04-14, superseded.**

The authoritative translation is in `../../translation/`, produced from
the NDL 古典籍OCR-Lite mechanical transcription in
`../../transcription/`.

This folder is retained for historical reference: some phrasing,
structural choices, and scholarly framing from the Phase 2 drafts may
remain useful input to the Phase 3 translation, but the Phase 2 drafts
inherited uncertainty and interpolation from their vision-LLM source
transcription and should not be cited as-is.
