### Right page: Position-Scaling Tree

〈A tree diagram with circled numbers (①②③④) in red ink:〉

```
五百両買
    ②────①
   / \
  ④   ③  千十(?)両買
```

〈READING NOTE on 千十両買:
The bottom label of the tree reads 千十両買. This could be:
(a) 千十両 = 1,010 ryō (literal reading)
(b) 千両 = 1,000 ryō with 十 as a separate annotation
(c) A cursive ligature where 十 is part of another character

The tex figure caption (line 535) renders this as "1,000-ryō base
purchase (千十両買)" — treating 千十 as 千 (1,000). This discrepancy
should be resolved. If the reading IS 千十両 (1,010), the caption
should say so; if it's 千両 (1,000), the transcription should note
the 十 differently.

FURTHER NOTE: The caption says "building FROM a 1,000-ryō base
purchase... TO a 500-ryō position" — this direction is confusing.
Looking at the tree, the numbered stages go ①→②→③→④, with ① at
the bottom (千十両買) and the top labeled 五百両買. The tree may
show how a 1,000-ryō capital base is deployed through buying
positions that max out at 500 ryō committed (with the rest held
in reserve per the staged entry method). Alternatively, the diagram
may read top-down rather than bottom-up.〉
