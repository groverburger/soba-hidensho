# Pages 21–24: The World-Levels Diagram and the Five-Stage Secrets of Strong and Weak Qi

These four pages carry the book's most concentrated quantitative
section: a ceremonial three-level diagram of the market's range
(世の中三段之図), followed by two mirror-image five-stage
accumulation schedules — the **Five-Stage Secret of Strong Qi**
(強気五ツの秘伝) for accumulating a long position into declines,
and the **Five-Stage Secret of Weak Qi** (弱気五ツの秘伝) for
distributing a short position into rallies. Each five-stage schedule
is presented first as a compact table of drawdown/rally percentages
and position sizes, then as a worked example on each stage.

---

## Page 21 — Closing of the Three Virtues; the World-Levels Diagram

The page opens with the final verses of Courage (the ten-verse
closing rank of the forty-eight-verse Three Virtues sequence that
began on page 18), then introduces the new section with national
agricultural figures and a large boxed diagram.

### ◯ Final verses of Courage (勇 — *Yū*)

> 居る理家皆、人〔一〕こと〔に〕桜の心〔を〕もる、大したのやまかひ
> の種まち。

*Every house that abides has its cherry-heart; at the great pole
of the hidden-mountain, one plants the seed of waiting.*

> 高下とも、一刻・五分の前なざやはいつでも米にむかふ理としれ。

*In either high or low, the prior spread of a one-step or five-*bu*
move is always the reason-to-face-the-rice. Know this.*

### 飛上不可御門　飛下可〈レ〉 — *On spikes up and spikes down*

*(Annotation: "Tenmei recension.")*

A boxed rule in classical Chinese with a *kundoku* return-mark:

> 飛さげはいつでも米にむかふべし。飛上げならば米に随へ。
>
> 飛上げの三日つゞきて音なくば、米にむかひて飛さげを聞きて。

*On a downward spike, always face the rice. On an upward spike,
follow the rice.*

*When an upward spike has run three days without an echo, face the
rice and wait for the downward spike to sound.*

> **智仁勇三徳の歌、終り。** 〔量絵等、考へ合すべし。〕

*Here ends the Song of the Three Virtues — Wisdom, Benevolence,
and Courage. Consider the diagrams and illustrations together.*

### Field Area and Total Rice (田地米の数目)

A small annotation in the margin: *「夜正者曰、順和名抄拾粉廿三才」*
— a reference, in the editor's hand, to the *Wamyō Ruiju-shō*
(*Ordered Dictionary of Japanese Names*, 10th c.).

> 惣高米、合せて **二千八百八万五千四百八十二石**。
>
> 田数、合せて **九十四万七千八百壹丁**。

*Total rice yield: **twenty-eight million, eight-hundred-fifty-
thousand, four-hundred-eighty-two koku*** (28,085,482 *koku*).

*Total field area: **nine-hundred-forty-seven thousand, eight-
hundred-and-one chō*** (947,801 *chō*).

*[These are the all-Japan agricultural reference figures the rest
of the method takes for granted; they frame the practical scale
at which the book is pitched.]*

## Page 22 — Three-level method worked out; the Five-Stage Secret of Strong Qi

### Continuation: how to apply the three-level method

> 右三段の法をもつて、其の年々の世の中の善悪を積る。
>
> 上分の年、三十年もつゞくことなり。下分の年、八九年もつゞくこと
> あり。

*Using the three-level method above, one accumulates the good-and-
bad of each year's world.*

*A year of the upper division may run on for thirty years; a year
of the lower division can run on for eight or nine.*

> 〇　さて、其の卑の出来を考ふること。中分の世の中時節、その年の
> 出来七分とつもらは、七に三をかけて見れば、三七廿一俵。これを
> 中墨の直〔段〕と見るべし。

*Consider the out-turn of the low ranking. In a middle-division
world, if the year's estimate is seven-tenths, multiply three times
seven — twenty-one tawara. Take this as the middle-ink price.*

*Side weights: 八斗四升 (0.84 *koku*), 七十六匁一分 (76.1 *monme*) —
the reference weight and price for the 21-tawara case.*

> 左様の物直段ならば、三割のさぶり、六俵をいれて廿六俵を〔中〕法
> とすべし。暮そのこしに変り申さぬ〔法〕にて、一厘も小違なり。

*For such a price: add six tawara of the thirty-percent drawdown
buffer to get twenty-six tawara as the middle law. At year-end,
this holds without variance; not even one hundredth-of-a-*monme*
off.*

> いつの歳にても、十二分・一分・三割、十二分・十一分・三割と差ふ
> べし。

*In any year, hold to the schedule: twelve-tenths and one-tenth at
thirty percent, twelve-tenths and eleven-tenths at thirty percent.*

Editor's gloss at the bottom, in small hand, attributed to **校正者**
(*the editor*): seven categories of the world-estimate — 上・中・下
— pair with the thirds / half / thirds-plus subdivisions to give the
full calibration scheme.

### ◯ The Five-Stage Secret of Strong Qi (強気五ツの秘伝)

*(Annotation: "Tenmei recension.")*

A compact **five-stage buy-on-dips schedule** — the heart of the
book's accumulation method. Read against a base established at the
year's **new-rice initial price as middle-ink** (新米初直段を中墨と
見る):

```
   ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │   Stage         Drawdown from middle-ink      Ryō to buy
   │   ─────         ──────────────────────────    ──────────
   │   第一  (1st)   一割半さげ   (15 % down)        百両  (100 ryō)
   │   第二  (2nd)   二割さげ     (20 % down)        二百両 (200 ryō)
   │   第三  (3rd)   二割半さげ   (25 % down)        四百両 (400 ryō)
   │   第四  (4th)   三割さげ     (30 % down)        八百両 (800 ryō)
   │   第五  (5th)   三割余       (over 30 %)       千両  (1 000 ryō)
   │                                                 ↑
   │                                         or up to 二千両 (2 000 ryō)
   └────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Editor's note:** 「此五段、〔いずれ〕にても天元商句なり。〔千両〕
にても二千両にても買ふべし。」 — *"These five stages are each a*
tengen *trade-maxim. Whether a thousand or two thousand *ryō*, buy
accordingly."*

> 第一：先ず、心に、其の年の世の中の十分と積り、新米の直段の
> ことのほか高く、慥に三割も下がるべしと思ふ年にても、必ず必ず
> 売るべからず。

*Stage One: First, reckon the year's world at ten-tenths. Even in
a year where the new-rice price is exceptionally high and you are
certain it ought to fall thirty percent — by no means sell.*

The progression: you are building a **long position** into declines.
The bigger the drawdown, the bigger the add — doubling from 100 to
200 to 400 to 800, then a final scale-out to 1000 or 2000 at the
extremity.

## Page 23 — The Strong-Qi Five Stages worked in detail

Each of the five stages is now presented with specific tawara counts,
weights, and prices. The schedule assumes a **middle-ink reference of
20 tawara** (中墨廿俵) and builds from there.

### Stage One — 一割半さげ, buy 100 ryō

> 一割半さげ〔に〕買ひ申し候〔こと〕が大秘密なり。いつの年にても、
> 新米初直段を中墨と見る — 家伝の秘密なり。

*To buy at the one-and-a-half-tenths drawdown is the great secret.
In any year, to take the new-rice opening price as the middle-ink —
this is the family secret.*

| Level              | Tawara | Weight              | Price       |
|--------------------|:------:|:--------------------|:------------|
| Middle-ink (中墨)   | 20     | —                   | —           |
| 1½-step down (一刻半) | 23    | 九斗四升 (0.94 *koku*) | 六十五匁五分 (65.5 *monme*) |
| Further             | 24    | 九斗六升 (0.96)      | 六十六匁一分 (66.1) |
| Second 100-ryō buy at 25 tawara | 25 | 一石 (1.0)        | 六十三匁五分 (63.5) |
| Third 200-ryō buy at 26 tawara | 26 | 一石四升 (1.04)     | 六十一匁五分 (61.5) |

**Totals after the first stage:**

> 四百両買 〆 八百両買持なり。七十俵の損。一割等がれば百六十俵
> の徳になる〔べし〕なり。

*400-*ryō* buy ⟹ 800-*ryō* long position held. Loss of 70 tawara.
Should the market rise one-tenth (ten percent), a profit of 160
tawara.*

### Stage Two — 二割さげ, double the size

| Level                | Tawara |
|----------------------|:------:|
| Middle-ink           | 20     |
| At 25 tawara, 二百両買 | 25     |
| At 26 tawara, 二百両  | 26     |
| — further cumulative buy |    |

*Cumulative: **八百両買持**. 60 tawara loss; +160 tawara on a 10%
rally.*

### Stage Three — 二割半さげ

| Level                            | Tawara |
|----------------------------------|:------:|
| Middle-ink down 2½ tenths (from 20) | 25 |
| Further: 四百両買                  | 26     |
| — cumulative: 一千四百両買〆八百両 |        |

*Loss of 40 tawara. On a one-step rally: +160 tawara.*

### Stage Four — 三刻さげ

*Three-step drawdown from the middle-ink.* Buys scaled through the
twenty-six-tawara level; cumulative loss narrowing further as the
position averages down.

### Stage Five — 三割余

At this stage, the drawdown has exceeded thirty percent from the
middle-ink. The book permits the 1000-to-2000 *ryō* commitment at
twenty-seven or twenty-eight tawara:

> 中墨廿俵本より三刻余もさぶり廿七八俵ならば、千両にでも二千両
> にでも買べし。一者の〔試〕ためしと思ふべし。

*From a middle-ink of 20 tawara, once the drawdown runs to three-
step-plus and you see 27 or 28 tawara — buy a thousand or even
two thousand *ryō*. Take it as the one-man's proof-of-concept.*

> 此〔の〕〔直段〕へのさげは、千に六と川の事なり。

*At such a level, the decline is a *six-and-stream* event — one
in a thousand.*

## Page 24 — The Five-Stage Secret of Weak Qi (弱気五ツの秘伝)

The mirror image of page 22's strong-qi schedule. Where strong-qi
builds a long into declines, **weak-qi builds a short into rallies**.

### ◯ 弱気五つの秘伝図 — *Diagram of the Five Stages of Weak Qi*

```
   ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │   Stage         Rally from middle-ink          Ryō to sell
   │   ─────         ─────────────────────────     ──────────
   │   第一  (1st)   一刻半あげ   (1½ step up)        百石  (100 koku)
   │   第二  (2nd)   二刻あげ     (2 step up)         二百両 (200 ryō)
   │   第三  (3rd)   二刻半あげ   (2½ step up)        四百両 (400 ryō)
   │   第四  (4th)   三刻あげ     (3 step up)         八百両 (800 ryō)
   │   第五  (5th)   三割余あげ   (over 30 %)        千両  (1 000 ryō)
   │                                                 ↑
   │                                         or up to 二千両
   └────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Editorial gloss: 「右同断、同同同壱人」 — *"same as above, one
and the same person."* The mirror structure means a single trader
runs the same schedule in the weak-qi direction.

### Stage One — 一刻半あげ, sell 100 koku

| Level                         | Tawara | Weight             | Price             |
|-------------------------------|:------:|:-------------------|:------------------|
| Middle-ink, starting level    | 17     | 六斗八升 (0.68)     | —                 |
| Reference weight              | —      | 大十九斗九分         | (margin note)     |
| 100-ryō sell at 16 tawara     | 16     | 五斗五俵?           | —                 |
| 100-ryō sell                  | —      | 九斗六升 (0.96)     | —                 |
| 200-ryō sell at 14 tawara     | 14     | 百十四丁 (weight)   | —                 |

> 引にて四百両売、七十俵の損。一刻さがる生〔あ〕れば、百六十俵の
> 徳なり。

*Cumulative 400-*ryō* sell; 70 tawara loss. A one-step decline
yields 160 tawara profit.*

### Stage Two — 二刻あげ

*Scales up proportionally:* middle-ink 20 → sells at 15 and 14 tawara
→ **1,400-*ryō* ⟹ 800-*ryō* short** after consolidation. 60 tawara
loss; 160 tawara gain per step decline.

### Stage Three — 二刻半あげ

*Middle-ink 20, sold-through-15, then **400-*ryō* sell ⟹ 800-*ryō*
short**. 40 tawara loss; 160 tawara gain per step decline.*

### Stage Four

*Continuing on the same cadence. Middle-ink 20 held. One-step
decline yields 160 tawara.*
