# Pages 25–28: The Seven Fortunes, Compound Trading Variants, and the Upper-Three / Lower-Three Diagram

These four pages present the book's remaining composite trading
schedules — the **strong-qi / weak-qi reverse-weight** (逆積),
**favorable-virtue reverse-loss** (順徳逆損), the **random-edge** and
**stake-use** (度端 / 立用) trades, and the **follow-the-color**
trade (随色) — and close the diagrammatic sequence with the **Third
Diagram**: the radial **Upper-Three / Lower-Three Diagram**
(上割三下割三之圖), the book's most elaborate single figure.

---

## Page 25 — Closing the weak-qi fifth stage; the Seven Fortunes; the Strong-Qi Reverse-Weight trade

### Closing the weak-qi five-stage schedule

The final stage of the weak-qi schedule (begun on page 24) closes at
the top of page 25:

> 中墨廿俵、〔元〕より三刻余もあがり、十二三俵ならば、千両にでも
> 二千両にでも、一書の道だめしとおもふべし。此のうへのあげとても、
> 千に六と川の上となり。

*From a middle-ink of 20 tawara, once the rally exceeds three steps
and the tawara-count drops to 12 or 13 — sell a thousand or two
thousand *ryō*; take it as the one-book's proof-of-method. Even the
further rise above this is a six-and-stream event — a single case
in a thousand.*

### ◯ The Seven Fortunes Secret Volume (七福即生秘密巻目)

*[OCR renders the header as `七福郎生秘密巻目`; the image shows
the standard form **七福即生秘密** (*Shichifuku sokushō hihō* —
"Secret of the Immediate Arising of the Seven Fortunes").]*

The section opens without a numbered list of the seven fortunes —
despite what a Phase 2 draft asserted. The OCR and the image both
show the section proceeding directly from the heading into an
enumeration of **trading variants**, each introduced by a red-circle
marker and a method-name. What the book calls **七福** (the seven
fortunes) is the name of the chapter that contains these compound
trade-schedules, not a list of seven separate conditions.

Opening instruction:

> 惣じて、唐端は三刻さげ・三刻ほどと申せども、買方には一刻の強み
> 相見え候へば、買方にすぐるゝ〔事〕なし。いつの年にても、新米初
> 直段を中墨と見て、買べし。

*In general, the Tang-edge is said to be three-step-down or three-
step-up; however, as long as the buying side shows a one-step
strength, there is no losing the buying side. In any year, take the
new-rice opening price as middle-ink, and buy.*

### ◯ Strong-Qi Reverse-Weight Trade (強気逆権商内)

> 逆積の商力〔は〕中墨あり。二刻までは一刻はなし。二刻余は五分
> なしなり。

*The reverse-weight trading force has its middle-ink. Up through
two steps there is no one-step; beyond two steps there is no
five-*bu*.*

A compact schedule follows — the trader builds a long position
across five scale-in levels, each slightly below the previous,
with the cumulative total reaching **一千六百両買持** (1,600-ryō
long held):

| Level                  | Tawara | Buy         |
|------------------------|:------:|:------------|
| Middle-ink (天元)      | 22     | 100-*ryō*   |
| Next step down         | 25     | 200-*ryō*   |
| Further 5 bu / 5 bu    | 26     | 400-*ryō*   |
| One-step-half area     | (same) | 800-*ryō*   |
| 30%-down / 4割-over    | —      | (scale-out) |

> 〔合せて〕一千六百両買持、百八十俵の損。一割あがくと三百廿俵
> の徳の徳。順徳の音〔かと〕。

*Cumulative: 1,600-ryō long held; 180 tawara drawdown. A ten-
percent rally yields 320 tawara gain — the voice of favorable
virtue.*

### ◯ Strong-Qi Ten-Percent-Up Favorable-Virtue Reverse-Loss Trade (強気一割あげ順徳逆損商内)

A five-way distribution schedule: **五方ならし** (*gohō-narashi* —
"five-direction smoothing"). From the middle-ink, as the market
**rises** the trader scales out in five graduated sells.

| Level           | Tawara     | Sell        |
|-----------------|:----------:|:------------|
| Middle-ink      | 20 (七斗四升) | 100-*koku* sell |
| Up ½ step       | 19 (七斗二升) | 100-*ryō* sell  |
| Up 1 step       | 18 (同)     | —           |
| Up 1½ step      | 17 (八斗四升) | —           |
| —               | 16         | —           |

> 御通し、五分一刻あがり。拾八俵、七斗二升、八十八匁。徳ナリ。
>
> 五分売次、廿俵の徳。

*Pass-through: at a five-*bu* one-step rally, 18 tawara at 0.72 *koku*,
88 *monme* — that's the profit. On the five-*bu* next-sell, 20
tawara profit.*

*[The 五方ならし label (five-direction smoothing) captures the
schedule's shape: five graduated sell-scale-outs across the rally,
balancing position exposure as the market climbs.]*

## Page 26 — Strong-Qi 1½-step-up and 2-step-up variants

### ◯ Strong-Qi 1½-Step-Up Favorable-Virtue Reverse-Loss Trade (強気一割半あげ順徳逆損商内)

The same structural pattern as page 25's ten-percent variant, but
keyed to a **15 % rally threshold** and executed across a tighter
tawara band.

| Stage           | Tawara       | Weight / Price        | Action        |
|-----------------|:------------:|:----------------------|:--------------|
| Middle-ink      | 20           | —                     | —             |
| Up              | 19           | —                     | 100-*ryō* buy |
| Continuing      | 18           | 八十八匁 (88 *monme*)    | — (selling scale-out at one-step half up) |
| Further up      | 17           | 九斗四升 (0.94 *koku*)   | new 100-*ryō* buy (into the 15%-up pivot) |
| At 1½-step rally | 16          | —                     | 100-*ryō* sell |
| At more rally    | 15          | —                     | 100-*ryō* sell |
| Further          | 14          | —                     | —             |

*Cumulative: **800-*ryō* sell**. 70 tawara drawdown at the turn;
160 tawara profit on a one-step decline.*

### ◯ Strong-Qi Two-Step-Up Favorable-Virtue and Ancillary Trade (強気二刻あげ順徳丞様商内)

The mirror of the previous, now keyed to a **two-step rally**:

| Stage                | Tawara | Weight         | Price             |
|----------------------|:------:|:---------------|:------------------|
| 1,100-*ryō* sell at 19 | 19   | 七十九匁五分 (79.5) | —                 |
| Middle-ink           | 20     | 七斗六升 (0.76)  | 八十四匁一分 (84.1)  |
| Further              | 18     | 七斗二升 (0.72)  | —                 |
| At 17 tawara         | 17     | 九十九匁一分 (99.1)| —                 |
| At 2-step rally      | 16     | —              | —                 |

> 順徳土斗四升、四拾俵の徳なり。則ち拾火俵、七斗四升にて、なり
> 四拾俵の徳なり。則ち〔格〕〔書〕俵。二度目、天元目。〔ダン〕〔より〕
> 転発〔して〕、〔越〕に二百両売。それあり、段々御がれば仕合なり。

*Favorable-virtue, .74 *koku*, 40 tawara gain. Equivalently: 17
tawara at .74 *koku* yields 40 tawara gain. At the frame-tawara
count, on the second pass, at the *tengen*-mark — reverse from the
level, and beyond it sell 200-*ryō*. This is the fortunate case;
when the market keeps climbing step by step, the result is
agreeable.*

> 追違〔ひ〕、こす人、〔欲〕からず。

*Those who keep at it and pull through: their desire is not
excessive.*

Closing two-line annotation: *三刻あげと同断。一故直売ハセザルナリ*
— *Same as the three-step-up case. (One reason the straight-sell is
not executed.)*

## Page 27 — The Eight-Hundred-Ryō sell; the random-edge, stake-use, and follow-the-color trades

### Closing on the 800-*ryō* sell

> 申す事ありにも、三拾五俵かまづまでもあぶり、一冊〔キ〕なして
> も、なほ又、四百両売〔なり〕、猶その上まで行き〔な〕く、杉〔十〕
> 四俵仕〔切〕打〔切〕汁にて、四百〔両〕三刻上り十二〔両〕売、
> 一分の〔ミコ〕二十〔リ〕。

*Even with things-to-say: up to thirty-five tawara of play, even
without making a single-book-worth — still, four-hundred-*ryō* sell;
even further up, beyond that, with the cedar-fourteen-tawara
payoff-cut, four-hundred-*ryō* at three-step up, twelve-*ryō* sell,
one-*bu* of the twenty-*ri* stream.*

> 〆八百両売なり。六拾俵の損。一刻〔さがれ〕ば、百六拾俵の徳なり。

*Cumulative: 800-*ryō* sell. 60 tawara drawdown. On a one-step
decline, 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ Random-Edge One-Six Trade (度端一六の商内)

> 中墨廿俵出来候つまり、三刻余もさぶり申す時か、三刻余もあげりく
> 候時は、常に百両位を安に立ててなきなひすべし。

*From a 20-tawara middle-ink, whenever the market slumps beyond
three steps — or, alternately, rallies beyond three steps — always
trade the one-hundred-*ryō*-sized lot at the low level.*

> 一、七千両〔千両〕も二千両も〔と〕思〔し〕召〔し〕て、〔書〕高か
> くべし。三割余のさぶりとても、千に一ツ。あぶりとても、千に山とつ
> の事なり。

*Even if thinking in terms of one thousand or two thousand *ryō*,
pack it in lightly. Even a thirty-percent-plus drawdown is only one
case in a thousand; even a thirty-percent-plus rally is only a
mountain-of-one in a thousand.*

### ◯ Stake-Use Trade (立用商内)

> 立用には、はゝるあきをいはず、末々の米の〔上〕下を聞へ〳〵より、
> 能々相考へ為かけ申す事なり。

*In the stake-use trade, you do not speak of the gap — rather, from
having heard the future ups and downs of rice, you reflect carefully
and stake accordingly.*

> とき〴〵平物違正申す事なり。立用はおほく商内をする者の為に大分
> まきこ之な〔きも〕、その道理は、五万両も拾万両にも買持など等る
> とき、〔売〕りたく思へども、売いだすと脇より追々て六七分も一
> 〔ケ〕〔月〕も追ふことあり。故に思ふやうに売りなされずければ、
> 此の〔立〕用となることは、心安くいとなき事なり。

*Even the flat-goods now and then correct themselves. The stake-use
is not much of a wrapping-in for those who trade in large size —
the reason being that when you hold a long of 50,000 or 100,000
*ryō*, even if you wish to sell, as soon as you do, the side-market
chases you down six or seven *bu* and can pursue you for up to a
month. Because you cannot then sell as you wished, the stake-use
trade is far from an easy business.*

### ◯ Follow-the-Color Trade (随色商内)

Editorial note: 禁止者曰く、「此の商力は、本体の商具等〔し〕」
— *the prohibitor says: "this trading-force is the same as the
essential trading-equipment."*

> 一、随色の商内の法は、〔眞〕なる〔覚〕〔も〕れ〔の〕は、格別の
> 事のも〔無き〕ときは、宣下相済まじ、とおもふに、米俄に五分方・
> 七分方もあがる、その〔木〕がる〔船〕を売せて、五分・七分・七分
> さがりける処を買べし。是を〔随〕色といふなり。

*The law of the follow-the-color trade: at those moments when the
genuine awareness is dulled — when nothing exceptional has occurred,
and one is inclined to say no imperial edict will come — rice
suddenly rallies five-*bu* or seven-*bu*. Have that ship sold, and
buy at the place where it retraces five-*bu*, seven-*bu*, seven-*bu*.
This is called follow-the-color.*

> しかし、百両か二百両〔を〕為かくべし。慥なる事もなきに、おほく
> 楽すぐるは、手荷の強気なるべし。其外、追よひ追々りなども、随分
> の商内なり。此の商内を**添子**すくこといふなり。

*Still, stake only a hundred or two hundred *ryō*. Without certainty,
to overdo the ease is the bold overhand of luggage. Beyond that,
following-each and trailing-each are themselves respectable trades.
This trade is called* adopted-child *entry.*

> 〔随色〕の文字に可見達と見立〔て〕、第一なるべし.

*Take the "follow-the-color" wording as what is to be held in
plain view — that is first.*

## Page 28 — The Secret of the Random Edge; the Third Diagram

### ◯ The Secret of the Random Edge — chapter 12 (度端極意の巻)

> 夫〔れ〕、度端といふきなひは、欲をきりて放つ〔こと〕を極意とす。
> 〔寸〕こし〔の〕〔空〕きよりおほいなるを順とは、米商力のもとなり。
> 故に一散せざれは、子座に千度等こるべからず。

*The trade called* random-edge *— cutting desire and releasing it is
its secret. That the greater-than-a-small-gap is the favorable — this
is the root of rice-trading force. Therefore, without dispersing once,
one cannot hope to rally a thousand times at the child's seat.*

### ◯ The Third Diagram — Upper-Three / Lower-Three (第三　上割三下割三之圖)
