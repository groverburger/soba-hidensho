# Pages 1–2: Cover and Inner Title Spread

## Page 1 — Front Cover

The cover is woodblock-printed in faded ink over patterned paper, now
worn and foxed. The main title runs vertically in bold characters down
the center; a small cartouche at the bottom bears the completeness
mark.

**Title (main cartouche):** 校正　三猿金泉秘録　全
(*Kōsei Sanen Kinsen Hiroku — Zen*) — *Revised Three Monkeys Gold
Spring Secret Record — Complete*.

**Library markings (upper left and lower right):** three overlaid
labels in handwritten ink — accession numbers and catalogue shelfmarks
from the Tanimura Collection (*Tanimura Bunko*, 谷村文庫) of Kyoto
University's Main Library. The OCR captures fragments (`谷村文`,
`廿九日`) but the labels are incidental to the text and are noted here
only for provenance.

*[OCR for page 1 contains extensive noise from the digitization color
calibration chart and the library-label handwriting; the above is the
content legibly rendered on the cover itself.]*

## Page 2 — Inner Title Spread

### Right side — Title block

**Small text, top:** 諸秘論首巻 (*Sho-hiron shukan*) — *Opening
Volume of the Collected Secret Treatises*.
*[OCR: 活法秘論首巻 → likely 諸秘論首巻. 諸 is a well-attested
confusion with 活法 in cursive script.]*

**Main title, large characters:** 校正　三猿金泉秘録　全
(*Kōsei Sanen Kinsen Hiroku — Zen*).

**Library accession stamp (modern ink, below title):** `91006836`.

**Red square seal (蔵書印, *zōshoin* — ownership seal), below the
accession stamp:** four characters in seal script, illegible at this
resolution.

### Left side — Donor inscription

In large flowing cursive across the left page:

> **嶋田君寄贈** (*Shimada-kun kizō*) — *Donated by Mr. Shimada*.

This is an ownership inscription in a later hand, unrelated to the
text's editor (鳴川猛之助 — Narukawa Takenosuke) whose name appears
on page 3. OCR reads the first character as 嶋; earlier vision-LLM
transcriptions read 鳴 and proposed the name *Naruta*. Machine OCR
favors 嶋田 (*Shimada*); we follow the OCR.

### Bottom-right corner — Ancillary label

A small rectangular label, oriented reversed relative to the book's
main text (visible when the back board is held upward), bears the
inscription **三猿軍書選** (*San-en Gunsho-sen*) — *Selection from
the Three Monkeys Strategic Writings* — or a close variant. The
middle characters (軍書 vs. 叢書) are small and partly obscured.

*[Page 2 OCR also returns spurious strings such as `第一著著著著著…`
and `アーサ`; these are artifacts of show-through ink from adjacent
leaves and library-label handwriting, not body text.]*
