# Pages 23–26: The five bullish and five bearish secrets; the seven fortunes; reverse-power and retained-profit trades

## Page 23 — The five bullish secrets, with figures

The rule common to all five:

> **Take the new rice's first-sale price as the *chūzumi* (中墨,
> reference).** This is the house's secret. For a worked example,
> suppose the *chūzumi* is **20 tawara**.

### ◯ 第一 — *First secret: the 1½-koku fall — buy 100 ryō*

> *"A one-*koku*-and-a-half fall is properly noted as the great
> secret. In any year, the new rice's first-sale price is the
> *chūzumi*."*

From *chūzumi* 20 tawara, down one-*koku* and a half, the step
table runs:

```
   ~65 momme 5 bu (9-to 4-shō)   23 tawara  ← sell side (above pivot)
   ~66 momme 1 bu (9-to 6-shō)   24 tawara  ← buy 100 ryō
   ── pivot / chūzumi ──          20 tawara
   ~63 momme 5 bu (1-koku 4-shō)  26 tawara  ← buy 200 ryō
   ~61 momme 5 bu                 25 tawara  ← buy 400 ryō
```

**Position at full extension**: 800 ryō held long, with a paper
loss of about 70 tawara. *On a one-*koku* rise, the gain is
160 tawara.*

### ◯ 第二 — *Second secret: the 2-koku fall — buy 200 ryō*

*Chūzumi* 20 tawara; step table descending:

```
   ~79 momme 9 bu                 (below chūzumi step)
   ~66 momme 1 bu (9-to 6-shō)    25 tawara  ← buy 100 ryō
   ── pivot ──                    20 tawara
                                  26 tawara  ← buy 200 ryō
```

**Full extension**: 800 ryō held, paper loss ~60 tawara. *One-*koku*
rise → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第三 — *Third secret: the 2½-koku fall — buy 400 ryō*

From the *chūzumi*, down two-and-a-half *koku*:

```
   chūzumi          20 tawara
   ikake            25 tawara   (65 momme region)
   ↓ buy 400 ryō    26 tawara
```

Total position: 1,400 ryō long, paper loss ~41 tawara. *One-*koku*
rise → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第四 — *Fourth secret: the 3-koku fall — buy 800 ryō*

```
   chūzumi          20 tawara
   3-koku fall      26 tawara  (≈ 60 momme 5 bu, 1-koku 6-shō)
   buy 800 ryō at this step
```

*One-*koku* rise → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第五 — *Fifth secret: over-3-koku fall — buy 1,000 ryō*

From *chūzumi* 20 tawara, *down more than three koku*, the price
reaches 27–28 tawara — *one-*koku* rise → 160 tawara profit* —

### ◯ 弱気五ツの秘伝図 — *The five secrets of the bear side*

A mirror of the bullish ladder: five rises, with a short-size
prescribed for each.

| # | Rise from the pivot | Sell size   |
|---|---------------------|-------------|
| 1 | 1-*koku*-and-a-half | 100 ryō     |
| 2 | 2-*koku*            | 200 ryō     |
| 3 | 2-*koku*-and-a-half | 400 ryō     |
| 4 | 3-*koku*            | 800 ryō     |
| 5 | over 3-*koku*       | 1,000 ryō   |

Editor's gloss: *"Same explanation as above, for the same single
person. Whether a thousand or two thousand, sell. The ten'gen
produces it."*

### ◯ 第一 — *First: the 1½-koku rise — sell 100 ryō*

*Chūzumi* 20 tawara; step table ascending (fewer tawara per ryō as
the price climbs):

```
   ── pivot ──              20 tawara
   1-koku rise              17 tawara        ← sell 100 ryō
   (1-koku rise again)      16 tawara        ← sell 100 ryō
   1½-koku rise             15 tawara        ← sell 200 ryō
   2-koku rise              14 tawara        ← sell 200 ryō
```

**Full extension**: 400 ryō short, paper loss ~70 tawara. *One-*koku*
fall → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第二 — *Second: the 2-koku rise — sell 200 ryō*

```
   chūzumi           20 tawara
   2-koku up         16 tawara    ← sell 100 ryō (99.9 momme region)
   (further up)      15 tawara    (5-to 6-shō, 114.1 momme region)
                     14 tawara    ← sell 200 ryō
```

Total **400 ryō short** (OCR `一貫四百両〆八百` — plausibly *1,400
units, totalling 800*). *One-*koku* fall → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第三 — *Third: the 2½-koku rise — sell 400 ryō*

*Chūzumi* 20, up 2½ *koku*:

```
   chūzumi           20 tawara
   2½-koku up        15 tawara   (7 momme, 5-to 6-shō)
                     14 tawara   (114.1 momme)
   → make 400 ryō short; total 800 ryō short
```

*One-*koku* fall → 160 tawara profit.*

## Page 25 — Fourth and fifth bearish secrets; the seven fortunes; the reverse-power trade

### ◯ 第四 — *Fourth: the 3-koku rise — sell 800 ryō*

*Chūzumi* 20; at a 3-*koku* rise the price stands at roughly 14
tawara. *One-*koku* fall → 160 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 第五 — *Fifth: over-3-koku rise — sell 1,000 ryō*

From *chūzumi* 20, *more than three koku up*, the price reaches
12–13 tawara. Whether a thousand or two thousand ryō, treat it as
a once-in-a-lifetime trial. Further rises of that magnitude occur
only six or seven times in a thousand.

### ◯ 七福即生秘密 の巻 — *Chapter of the immediate arising of the seven fortunes*

[Emended from OCR `七福郎生秘密巻目` to `七福即生秘密（の）巻` per
manuscript page image (p. 25): the middle character is 即 (*soku*,
"immediate / at once"), not 郎. The compound 即生 (*sokushō*) is
standard Buddhist vocabulary for "immediate arising."]

In the round, the *karahashi* (唐端) extends three *koku* high and
three *koku* low — so it is said. But the buy side carries a
**一刻の強み** (*ichi-koku no tsuyomi*), a one-*koku* strength, and
so the buy side is never excessive. In any year, take the new
rice's first-sale price as the *chūzumi* and **buy**.

### ◯ 強気逆積商内 — *Bull-side reverse-accumulation trading*

[Emended from OCR `強気逆権商内` to `強気逆積商内` per manuscript
page image (p. 25): the middle character is 積 (*seki*,
"accumulate"), not 権. The section opens *"逆積の商内…"* and the
prose describes a reverse-*accumulation* ladder, which confirms
the reading.]

*Reverse-accumulated* trading: working off the *chūzumi*, up to a
two-*koku* move there is no one-*koku* step used — beyond two
*koku* there is not even a 5-*bu* step.

Worked table (dense in the OCR; approximate):

```
   chūzumi    20 tawara    (75 momme region)     — buy 100 ryō at ten'gen
                22 tawara    (72 momme 7 bu)      — further
                                                   (9-to 6-shō, 76 momme)
   11-month    24 tawara    — (1-koku half rise at reference)
                                   ── intermediate ──
                25 tawara    — buy 200 ryō
                26 tawara    — buy 400 ryō
                             (1-koku 4-shō, 61 momme 5 bu;
                              5-bu down + 5-bu down → 1-koku total
                              ≡ 3-wari range)
                  buy 800 ryō at this step
```

**Full extension**: 1,600 ryō held, paper loss of 180 tawara; *on a
one-*koku* rise, 320 tawara profit.*

### ◯ 強気一割あげ順徳逆損商内 — *Bull-side 1-koku rise: the virtue-kept / loss-levelled trade*

An opening example — a *gohō narashi* (五方ならし, five-way
levelling). From ten'gen, selling 100 *koku* at a chūzumi of 20
tawara:

```
   ten'gen            20 tawara    (chūzumi; starting long-hold)
   19 tawara          (7-to 8-shō, 84 momme 1 bu) — sell 100 ryō
   ↑ 5-bu / 1-koku rise → 18 tawara (7-to 2-shō, 88 momme)
                                      sell 200 ryō (profit side)
   ↑ 1-koku half rise → 16 tawara    (99.9 momme)
```

## Page 26 — Continuation of the virtue-kept / loss-levelled trade

From the previous step, the ladder continues:

```
   16 tawara            sell 200 ryō, 1-koku half rise
   14 tawara            (5-to 6-shō, 114 momme 1 bu)
                        sell 200 ryō; makes 400 ryō total short
   ─────── total: 800 ryō short, 70 tawara paper loss
                  on a 1-koku fall → 160 tawara profit ────
   (if instead rising to 19 tawara: 5-bu rise above ten'gen;
    a one-momme step is a 1-koku gain)
```

**Bull-side retained-profit extension (順徳 shift)**:

```
   ten'gen    22 tawara    buy 100 ryō
   ten'gen    23 tawara    (9-to 4-shō, 69 momme 5 bu) — +1-koku sell
   ten'gen    24 tawara    (9-to ·, 66 momme 1 bu) — +1½-koku sell
   ten'gen    25 tawara    (1-koku, 63 momme 5 bu) — +2-koku sell
      ── 800 ryō short; 160-tawara paper loss;
         1-koku rise → 320-tawara profit ──
```

### ◯ 強気一割半あげ順徳逆損商内 — *Bull-side 1½-koku rise: the virtue-kept / loss-levelled trade*

Starting from a bought ten'gen:

```
   chūzumi    20 tawara    (84 momme 1 bu)              100 ryō buy
              19 tawara    (7-to 2-shō, 88 momme 8 bu)   — flat move
              18 tawara    (another 5-bu rise, ½-koku ahead)
                           → sell 100 ryō in 2 installments (20 tawara profit)
              17 tawara    (1-koku rise; twice the 1½-koku rise registered)
                           → new rice buy, 100 ryō
                           → 16 tawara: sell 100 ryō
                           → 15 tawara: sell 100 ryō (5-bu below ten'gen)
              14 tawara    (106 momme region, a further 5-bu)
                           → total 400 ryō sold
                           → short stance: 800 ryō
              ── 70 tawara paper loss;
                 1-koku fall → 160 tawara profit ──
```

### ◯ 強気二刻あげ順徳逆損商内 — *Bull-side 2-koku rise variant*

Same shape as the preceding, extended by one rung. Starting from
*chūzumi* 20 tawara at 84 momme 1 bu:

```
   chūzumi  20 tawara     100 ryō buy at 1,100 (context: ten'gen)
              19 tawara   (79 momme 5 bu)        — flat
              18 tawara   (7-to 2-shō, 88 momme) — +½-koku
              17 tawara   (9-to 4-shō)           — +1-koku, 20 tawara profit
              16 tawara   (99 momme 1 bu)        — +1½-koku
                           → sell 100 ryō
              (further rise, 2-koku move registered at ten'gen)
   Second pass from ten'gen: descending tenpen re-entry at
                 200 ryō sell, adding to the earlier 400 ryō short.
   ── if a three-*koku* move registers, a direct sell is not
      made (i.e. stand pat); if the move unwinds, each 5-bu of
      recovery restores a rung. ──
```

*[Interpretive. OCR for this section is dense with numeric
fragments (`天久三年`, `二度目天元目`, `二州上リテ天井ゟ見積`,
`二刻上リテ` …). The core teaching is clear: at the 2-*koku*
bull-side rise, the retained-profit trade holds its short
position, scales the ladder with symmetrical entries around the
*ten'gen*, and never sells on a pure direct (*jiki-uri*, 直売).]*

## Confidence

**High confidence**: the two five-step ladders (五 bullish / 五
bearish); the common rule *"take the new rice's first-sale price
as the* chūzumi" as the first secret; the section-titles
**七福即生秘密の巻**, **強気逆積商内**, **強気一割あげ順徳逆損
商内** — all verified against the manuscript images (p. 25) and
carrying corrections from the OCR (`七福郎生 → 七福即生`, `逆権
→ 逆積`).

**Medium confidence**: the individual rungs of each ladder. The
tawara / momme / ryō values are legibly present in the OCR, but
the pairing of each figure to the correct rung required
reconstruction from the column-of-numbers layout. The totals and
profit figures at the foot of each ladder (70-tawara paper loss /
160-tawara profit on a 1-*koku* move) are consistent across the
five worked examples, which gives a structural check.

**Lower confidence / interpretive**: the continuation of the
**強気一割半あげ順徳逆損商内** and **強気二刻あげ順徳逆損商内**
variants on p. 26. The OCR for these pages carries numerous
fragments (`天久三年`, `二度目天元目`, `一刻半ナリ`, `壱通し`)
whose placement in the original ladder is uncertain. The *sense*
of each variant — stepped entries of matched size, symmetrical
around the pivot — is stable; the exact arithmetic by rung should
not be cited without a cross-check against the image.
